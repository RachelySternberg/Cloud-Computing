JENI'S POWELL

Catty-corner to Kraft House No. 5 (and near Paris Flea Market and the Antique Mall), our Powell scoop shop is in the center of Powell, a charming small town neighborhood dotted with great independently-owned-and-operated businesses. Our shop, which features an elegant bar made of marble and reclaimed barn wood where customers can sit and enjoy their frozen confectionery, is our most spacious location (2,200 square feet).
