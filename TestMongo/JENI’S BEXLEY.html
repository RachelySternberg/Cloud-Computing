JENI’S BEXLEY

The shop we opened in June 2008 in the Bexley Gateway near Capital University is part of a charming storefront neighborhood that features art galleries, several destination dinner spots, and the illustrious Drexel East movie theater. Our Bexley hub, awash in natural light thanks to huge west-facing windows, is a great spot year round to enjoy a scoop with friends and family.
