JENI'S EASTON

Our shop at Easton Town Center, one of the Midwest’s most-popular spots to shop, eat and drink, and just kick back and watch the people do their thing, opened in May, 2013. The shop, next to the Lego Store, is in great company all year at Easton, which includes tenants such as Anthropologie, Nordstrom, and Apple, as well as Columbus-based favorites Northstar Cafe and Homage.
